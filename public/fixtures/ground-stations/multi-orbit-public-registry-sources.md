## Schema notes & non-claims

- **Generic dataset**: this registry contains no customer-specific tagging or
  branding strings; the data is intended for a generic multi-orbit viewer demo.
- **No fabricated stations**: every entry has a primary URL above and is
  filtered to ≥2 of {LEO, MEO, GEO}.
- **No commercial pair routing**: this registry asserts location +
  publicly-documented multi-orbit capability only. Whether station A actually
  exchanges traffic with station B for any specific service is commercial
  secret and out of scope.
- **Coordinate precision policy** matches the project's
  `m8a-v4-ground-station-projection.v1` doctrine: where exact site coordinates
  are classified or not publicly disclosed, the registry lists the
  municipality / operator-family region center and tags
  `disclosurePrecision: "operator-family-region"`.
